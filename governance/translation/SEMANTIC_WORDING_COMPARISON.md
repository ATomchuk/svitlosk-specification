# 1. Wording Comparison Table

|---------|-----------|-----------|-----------|
| Core phrase | "governing their complete publication lifecycle" | "governing the complete lifecycle of those Publications" | YES |
| Pronoun | "their" | "those Publications" | YES — explicit |
| Lifecycle reference | "publication lifecycle" | "lifecycle of those Publications" | YES — matches Glossary §11 |
| Entity ownership | Implicit | Explicit | YES |
| Ambiguity level | MEDIUM | LOW | YES |

---

# 2. Why v4 Is Better

1. **No ambiguous pronoun** — "those Publications" is explicit, "their" is not
2. **Matches repository pattern** — "lifecycle of [entity]" is the established Glossary pattern
3. **Explicit entity ownership** — "of those Publications" clearly assigns lifecycle to the entities
4. **Eliminates process ambiguity** — "publication lifecycle" could mean process; "lifecycle of those Publications" cannot
