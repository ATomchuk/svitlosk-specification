# DATA_MODEL

## Purpose

This document defines the official data model of the SvitloSK system.

All components of the system MUST use the entities and relationships defined in this specification.

---

## Scope

This specification applies to:

- data collection;
- data normalization;
- data storage;
- data publication;
- data archiving;
- analytics.

---

## Source of Truth

The official data source SHALL remain the only source of truth.

SvitloSK MUST NOT modify, reinterpret or generate official operational information.

The system MAY enrich official information with metadata required for processing.

---

# Core Entities

## Community

Administrative territory served by the system.

Current implementation:

- Starokostiantyniv Territorial Community

---

## Administrative Center

Primary settlement of the community.

Current implementation:

- Starokostiantyniv

---

## Elder District

Administrative subdivision of the community.

An Elder District SHALL contain one or more settlements.

---

## Settlement

City or village belonging to the Community.

---

## Street

Official street name within a Settlement.

---

## Address

The minimum geographical unit processed by the system.

Address consists of:

- Settlement
- Street
- Building Number

---

## Planned De-energization

Officially announced scheduled interruption.

Attributes SHALL include:

- publication date;
- start time;
- end time;
- affected addresses;
- source reference.

---

## Emergency De-energization

Officially announced emergency interruption.

Attributes SHALL include:

- publication timestamp;
- affected addresses;
- source reference.

---

## Schedule

Official schedule describing electricity availability by Subqueue.

Each Schedule SHALL contain:

- date;
- subqueue identifier;
- availability intervals.

---

## Publication

Information generated by the Publisher module.

Publication types:

- Planned De-energization;
- Emergency De-energization;
- Tomorrow Information;
- Tomorrow Schedule;
- Weekly Analytics.

---

## Daily Snapshot

Normalized representation of all operational data for one calendar day.

Daily Snapshot SHALL become immutable after archival.

---

## Analytics Record

Derived statistical information.

Analytics MUST NOT modify operational data.

---

# Data Categories

Operational Data

Current information received from official sources.

Historical Data

Archived operational information.

Analytical Data

Information generated from historical datasets.

---

# Data Lifecycle

Every dataset SHALL pass through the following lifecycle:

Collected

↓

Validated

↓

Normalized

↓

Published

↓

Updated (if required)

↓

Archived

---

---

# Data Integrity

Every entity processed by the system MUST be:

- identifiable;
- traceable;
- reproducible;
- timestamped;
- linked to its official source.

---

# Compatibility

Future extensions of the data model SHOULD preserve backward compatibility.

Breaking changes MUST be introduced only through the RFC process.
