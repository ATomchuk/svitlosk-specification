# Purpose

Define the editorial rules for all Ukrainian translations.

---

# 1. Document Titles

Ukrainian document titles SHALL be direct translations of English titles.

Do NOT add Ukrainian subtitles or parenthetical explanations in titles.

Example:

- English: "Telegram Semantic Model"
- Ukrainian: "Семантична модель Телеграм"

---

# 2. Headings

Section headings SHALL be translated.

Section numbering SHALL remain identical to the English source.

Do NOT reorder sections.

---

# 3. Lists

Bullet points and numbered lists SHALL be translated.

List formatting SHALL remain identical.

---

# 4. Tables

Table headers SHALL be translated.

Table structure SHALL remain identical.

Column widths may be adjusted for Ukrainian text length.

---

# 5. Abbreviations

| English | Ukrainian | Notes |
|---------|-----------|-------|
| SSP | SSP | NOT translated — proper identifier |
| TJS | TJS | NOT translated — proper identifier |
| DTS | DTS | NOT translated — proper identifier |
| PROC | PROC | NOT translated — proper identifier |
| ADR | ADR | NOT translated — proper identifier |
| KB | KB | NOT translated — proper identifier |
| RFC | RFC | NOT translated — international standard |

---

# 6. RFC 2119 Wording

RFC 2119 keywords SHALL remain in English.

Do NOT translate: SHALL, SHOULD, MAY, MUST, REQUIRED, PROHIBITED.

This follows international documentation standards and DTS-001.

---

# 7. Punctuation

Ukrainian punctuation rules apply.

Use Ukrainian quotation marks: " " (not " ").

Use Ukrainian dash: — (not -).

---

# 8. Capitalization

Follow Ukrainian capitalization rules.

Do NOT capitalize common nouns.

Do capitalize proper nouns and document IDs.

---

# 9. Quotation Style

Use double quotes for quotations: " текст ".

Do NOT use single quotes for quotations.

---

# 10. Formatting Consistency

Markdown formatting SHALL remain identical to the English source.

Code blocks SHALL NOT be translated.

File paths SHALL NOT be translated.

---

# 11. Terminology Requiring Owner Approval

The following terms have MEDIUM ambiguity risk and require explicit project-owner approval before translation is finalized.

---

## Publication Engine

- English Term: Publication Engine
- Recommended Translation: Движок публікацій
- Alternative Translation(s): Механізм публікацій, Система публікацій
- Reasoning: "Движок" is commonly used in Ukrainian IT for "engine." "Механізм" is more formal. "Система" is broader.
- Decision: **STATUS: PENDING OWNER APPROVAL**

---

## Canonical Equality

- English Term: Canonical Equality
- Recommended Translation: Канонічна рівність
- Alternative Translation(s): Канонічна тотожність, Стандартна рівність
- Reasoning: "Рівність" is the mathematical/logical term. "Тотожність" implies strict identity. Context is equality of canonical sources.
- Decision: **STATUS: PENDING OWNER APPROVAL**

---

## Powered

- English Term: Powered (as in "Powered Interval")
- Recommended Translation: Забезпечений електроенергією
- Alternative Translation(s): З живленням, Електрифікований
- Reasoning: "Забезпечений електроенергією" is precise but long. "З живленням" is shorter but less specific. "Електрифікований" implies permanent electrification.
- Decision: **STATUS: PENDING OWNER APPROVAL**
