# 2. Historical Terms

## 2.1 Edition

### Origin

Newspaper/magazine publishing.

### Classical Meaning

One print run of a publication.

### SvitloSk Usage

One daily instance of the Journal.

### Fit

**PARTIAL FIT** — Edition implies a fixed, finalized product. SvitloSk editions are dynamic and may be updated throughout the day.

### Evidence

> "One journal edition may include: planned outages (today)..." (Architect Intent)

---

## 2.2 Issue

### Origin

Magazine/serial publishing.

### Classical Meaning

One numbered publication in a series.

### SvitloSk Usage

One daily instance of the Journal (TELEGRAM_LIFECYCLE).

### Fit

**PARTIAL FIT** — Issue implies a numbered, sequential product. SvitloSk issues are not numbered.

### Evidence

> "An Issue opens when the first Publication for a calendar day is generated." (TELEGRAM_LIFECYCLE.md)

---

## 2.3 Release

### Origin

Software publishing.

### Classical Meaning

One version of a software product.

### SvitloSk Usage

One daily instance of the Journal (CASE-PUB-001).

### Fit

**POOR FIT** — Release implies a versioned, static product. SvitloSk releases are dynamic and not versioned.

### Evidence

> "Release is a COMPOSITE ENTITY." (CASE-PUB-001)

---

## 2.4 Publication

### Origin

General publishing.

### Classical Meaning

The act of making public.

### SvitloSk Usage

A public information message.

### Fit

**GOOD FIT** — Publication accurately describes a public information message.

### Evidence

> "Publication: A public information message generated by the SvitloSk system." (GLOSSARY §3)

---

## 2.5 Archive

### Origin

Library/archival science.

### Classical Meaning

Permanent storage of historical records.

### SvitloSk Usage

Historical storage of publications.

### Fit

**GOOD FIT** — Archive accurately describes permanent historical storage.

### Evidence

> "Historical Archive: The persistent storage of historical Interpretation Results and Publications." (GLOSSARY §2)

---

## 2.6 Journal

### Origin

Periodical publishing.

### Classical Meaning

A daily record of events.

### SvitloSk Usage

The ongoing publication service.

### Fit

**GOOD FIT** — Journal accurately describes an ongoing publication service.

### Evidence

> "The SvitloSk Public Information Journal is the primary public communication channel." (CHARTER §10)
