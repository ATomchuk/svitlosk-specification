**Document ID:** DRAFT-Q-002

**Title:** Can Issue Exist Without Publication?

**Status:** Partially Answered

---

# Question

Can Issue exist without Publication, or is Issue fundamentally dependent on Publication?

---

# Importance

HIGH — affects ontological hierarchy and concept dependency.

---

# Dependencies

- KF-006 (Issue Is Derivative)
- CASE-002A (Issue Hearing)
- CASE-002B (Issue Object vs Process)

---

# Current Hypotheses

1. Issue CANNOT exist without Publication (derivative)
2. Issue CAN exist without Publication (independent)
3. Issue existence depends on context (sometimes yes, sometimes no)

---

# Evidence

- CASE-002A: "Issue cannot exist without Publication"
- CASE-002B: "Issue is triggered by Publication"
- TJS-021 §8.1: "Issue opens when first Publication generated"
- TJS-000 §4: "Publication represents one independent publication belonging to an Issue"

---

# Partially Answered

TJS-021 §8.1 implies Issue requires Publication to open. But TJS-000 §4 implies Issue exists independently (Publication "belongs to" Issue). This circularity is unresolved.
