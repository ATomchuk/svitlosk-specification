# 2. Candidate Glossary

## 2.1 Core Concepts

### SvitloSk

A local public information service for residents of the Starokostiantyniv Territorial Community.

Provides understandable, timely and structured information about the current state of electricity supply.

Designed as a SOCIAL INFORMATION SERVICE rather than a technical utility.

**Source:** Architect Intent, CHARTER.md §1

---

## 2.2 Information Domains

### Queue Schedule

Official emergency electricity limitation based on the official Oblenergo schedule.

Consists of 6 queues and 12 subqueues.

NOT address-based.

Schedule changes by hour.

**Source:** Architect Intent

---

### Planned Outages

Address-based official planned maintenance.

Contains settlements, streets, houses, and planned time intervals.

Independent from Queue Schedule.

**Source:** Architect Intent

---

### Emergency Outages

Address-based unexpected outages.

Contains settlements, streets, houses, and emergency information.

Independent from Queue Schedule and Planned Outages.

**Source:** Architect Intent

---

## 2.3 Electricity Objects

### Queue

An official electricity distribution queue published by the Distribution System Operator.

Atomic object. Cannot be decomposed.

**Source:** GLOSSARY.md §5, Architect Intent

---

### Subqueue

A subdivision of an official Queue.

Atomic object. Cannot be decomposed.

Current implementation supports 12 subqueues.

**Source:** GLOSSARY.md §5, Architect Intent

---

### Schedule

Logical representation of electricity availability for one operational period.

Atomic object. Cannot be decomposed.

References one Subqueue.

**Source:** DATA_MODEL.md, Architect Intent

---

### Planned Outage

An officially announced scheduled interruption of electricity supply.

Atomic object. Cannot be decomposed.

Address-based. References Settlement, Street, House, Planned Time Interval.

**Source:** GLOSSARY.md §5, Architect Intent

---

### Emergency Outage

An unplanned interruption of electricity supply published by the official Data Source.

Atomic object. Cannot be decomposed.

Address-based. References Settlement, Street, House, Emergency Information.

**Source:** GLOSSARY.md §5, Architect Intent

---

## 2.4 Journal Concepts

### Journal

An ongoing service that continuously publishes information about electricity supply.

Produces Journal Editions over time.

**Source:** CHARTER.md §10, Architect Intent

---

### Journal Edition

One daily instance of the Journal.

Composite object containing multiple Publications organized by Territory.

**Source:** Architect Intent

---

### Publication

A public information message generated by the SvitloSk system.

Atomic object. Cannot be decomposed.

The smallest indivisible unit of information in the Journal.

**Source:** GLOSSARY.md §3

---

## 2.5 Publishing Concepts

### Publishing Subsystem

A service responsible for publishing the Journal through adapters.

**Source:** Architect Intent

---

### Telegram Adapter

A channel-specific implementation for Telegram.

Peer to Facebook Adapter. Neither is primary.

**Source:** Architect Intent

---

### Facebook Adapter

A channel-specific implementation for Facebook.

Peer to Telegram Adapter. Neither is primary.

**Source:** Architect Intent

---

## 2.6 PWA Concepts

### Timeline

A composite representation displaying multiple Schedules over time for one Subqueue.

**Source:** GLOSSARY.md §5, Architect Intent

---

### Tomorrow Graph

A composite representation displaying Schedules for all 12 Subqueues for tomorrow.

**Source:** Architect Intent

---

### Push Notification

An atomic representation notifying about electricity state change for one Subqueue.

Belongs ONLY to Queue Schedule domain.

**Source:** Architect Intent

---

### Address Lookup

A process that maps addresses to subqueues.

Takes Settlement, Street, House as input. Returns Subqueue assignment.

**Source:** Architect Intent

---

### Personal Cabinet

A composite object containing configuration items for the resident.

Includes two independent locations, push notification settings, silence mode, etc.

**Source:** Architect Intent

---

## 2.7 Territorial Concepts

### Community

The territorial community served by SvitloSk.

Top-level territorial unit.

**Source:** TERRITORIAL_MODEL.md

---

### Territory

A publication unit representing either the Administrative Centre or one Starosta District.

**Source:** GLOSSARY.md §4

---

### Starosta District (SO)

A territorial subdivision of the Community.

**Source:** GLOSSARY.md §4, TERRITORIAL_MODEL.md
