# 1. Purpose

# 2. Case Scope

## 2.1 What This Case Investigates

The ONTOLOGY of information products produced by SvitloSk.

NOT Publisher implementation.

ONLY the ontology of information products.

## 2.2 What This Case Does NOT Investigate

- Publisher implementation
- Lifecycle implementation
- SvitloSk architecture
- PWA integration
- Other future channels

## 2.3 Investigation Constraint

Publisher reconstruction must be based ONLY on Telegram + Facebook publication architecture.

Do NOT investigate PWA integration or other future channels.

# 4. Primary Research Question

**What exactly are the INFORMATION PRODUCTS produced by SvitloSk?**

---

# 5. Research Goals

## 5.1 Product Identification

Determine which information products exist.

## 5.2 Product Classification

For each product determine:

- Atomic or composite?
- Temporary or persistent?
- Channel-independent or channel-specific?
- Will it exist for Facebook?

## 5.3 Publication Analysis

- Is "Publication" the atomic product?
- Are there multiple publication product types?

## 5.4 Product Investigation

Independently investigate:

- Emergency publication
- Planned publication
- Tomorrow publication
- Queue graph publication
- Technical publication
- Service publication (if exists)

For every product determine:

- Identity
- Purpose
- Lifetime
- Owner
- Update behaviour
- Expiration behaviour
- Channel independence
- Relationship to Journal Edition
