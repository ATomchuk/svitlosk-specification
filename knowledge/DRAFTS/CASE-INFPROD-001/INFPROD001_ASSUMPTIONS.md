# 2. Hidden Assumptions

## 2.1 ASSUMPTION-001: Publication is the Atomic Unit

### Assumption

Publication is the smallest indivisible unit of information.

### Evidence

> "Publication: A public information message generated by the SvitloSk system." (GLOSSARY §3)

### Challenge

Queue Graph is NOT a Publication — it is an IMAGE.

Is the image a "publication" or a "representation"?

### Status

HIDDEN — assumption not explicitly stated.
