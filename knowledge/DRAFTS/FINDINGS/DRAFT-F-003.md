# DRAFT-F-003_Publication_Bridges_Information_Representation

**Document ID:** DRAFT-F-003

**Title:** Publication Bridges Information and Representation

**Status:** Draft

---

# Statement

Publication is the boundary between Information (content) and Representation (form). Publication is Information structured for distribution.

---

# Reasoning

The bridging role of Publication was discovered through semantic chain analysis:

**Observation 1: Information Has No Identity**

- Information is a relation between Reality and Knower
- Information has no lifecycle, no ownership
- Information cannot be pointed to (no Identity)
- Information is abstract, not concrete

**Observation 2: Representation Has No Content**

- Representation is the FORM of Information
- Representation is empty without Information
- Representation is platform-specific
- Representation borrows identity from what it represents

**Observation 3: Publication Has Both**

- Publication contains Information (content)
- Publication is Represented as Telegram Message (form)
- Publication has its own Identity (Territory + Date + Template)
- Publication bridges the abstract (Information) and the concrete (Representation)

**Observation 4: Publication Position in Chain**

- Publication is created from Interpretation Result (Information)
- Publication is rendered as Telegram Message (Representation)
- Publication is BETWEEN Information and Representation
- Publication is the transition point

# Possible Alternatives

**Alternative A:** Publication is just Information.

**Why rejected:** Publication has Identity, Lifecycle, Ownership — Information has none of these.

**Alternative B:** Publication is just Representation.

**Why rejected:** Representation is empty without Information; Publication contains Information.

**Alternative C:** Publication is a separate concept unrelated to Information and Representation.

**Why rejected:** Publication is created FROM Information and rendered AS Representation. It bridges them.
