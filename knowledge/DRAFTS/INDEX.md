**Why:** Every important conclusion from investigations needs to survive. Not just validated findings, but all significant conclusions.

**Why:** Every competing model discovered must survive. Even rejected models contain architectural insight.

## PRINCIPLES/

**Why:** Implicit principles discovered during investigations guide future work.

## QUESTIONS/

**Why:** Unresolved questions must never disappear. They guide future research.

**Content:** Question, importance, dependencies, hypotheses.

## REASONING/

**Why:** The reasoning behind conclusions is often more valuable than the conclusions themselves. This is the most important directory.

**Content:** Complete reasoning chains for every significant architectural insight.

# Archive Rules

1. One idea per file
2. Full reasoning preserved (never shortened)
3. Every file links to origin investigations
4. Every file links to related drafts
5. Status is always "Draft" (never canonical)
6. Architect decision field is always empty
