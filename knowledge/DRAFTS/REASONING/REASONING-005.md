# REASONING-005_Information_Not_Object

**Document ID:** REASONING-005

**Title:** Why Information Is Not an Object

**Status:** Draft

---

# Reasoning Chain

## Step 1: Information Requires a Knower

- Information is a relation between Reality and Knower
- Without a Knower, there is only Reality, not Information
- Information cannot exist independently

---

## Step 2: Objects Exist Independently

- Objects have identity that persists over time
- Objects can exist without observers
- Objects have lifecycle and ownership

---

## Step 3: Information Has No Identity

- Information is a relation, not a thing
- Information has no lifecycle
- Information has no ownership
- Information cannot be pointed to

---

## Step 4: Information Is Not Concrete

- Information is abstract (a relationship)
- Objects are concrete (things with identity)
- Information cannot be instantiated like an Object

---

## Step 5: Information Is Different from Information Object

- Information is the abstract concept (relation)
- Information Object is a concrete thing containing Information
- Publication is an Information Object, not Information itself

---

# Conclusion

Information is not an Object because it has no identity, no lifecycle, no ownership, and cannot exist independently. Information is a relation between Reality and Knower, not a thing.
