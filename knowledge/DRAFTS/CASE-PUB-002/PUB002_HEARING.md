# 1. Purpose

# 2. Case Scope

## 2.1 What This Case Investigates

The Telegram publishing subsystem — its operations, responsibilities, interfaces, and reusable logic.

## 2.2 What This Case Does NOT Investigate

- Parser A and Parser B (out of scope)
- Facebook (does not exist yet)
- New architecture design
- Specification modification

## 2.3 Investigation Constraint

This is a scientific reverse-engineering investigation.

The Publisher MUST be reconstructed FROM Telegram.

NOT invented.

---

# 3. Context

## 3.1 Reference Reconstruction Principle

> A reference architecture MUST NOT be invented.
> It MUST be reconstructed from an existing working implementation through systematic investigation, responsibility extraction, interface extraction, and generalization.

## 3.2 Existing Facts

- Current implementation contains only one real publishing implementation: Telegram
- Facebook does NOT exist yet
- Publisher exists only implicitly
- Therefore Publisher MUST be reconstructed FROM Telegram

## 3.3 Additional Context

- Current Telegram receives textual information generated by existing parsers
- Example source: today.txt from OutagesSk repository
- This file is NOT the Publisher — it is only one information source
- Current parsers remain OUT OF SCOPE

---

# 4. Research Tasks

## Research 1 — Operation Inventory

Identify every operation currently performed by Telegram.

## Research 2 — Operation Analysis

For every operation determine: why it exists, what input/output, whether channel-specific.

## Research 3 — Responsibility Extraction

For every operation determine: does this belong to Telegram Adapter, Publisher, Lifecycle, Journal, or another component.

## Research 4 — Hidden Coupling

Find every place where Telegram currently performs work that is actually unrelated to Telegram.

## Research 5 — Interface Extraction

For every Telegram responsibility identify: could another channel perform exactly the same operation?

## Research 6 — Reusable Logic

Identify logic that is channel independent, publication independent, reusable.

## Research 7 — Telegram-Specific Logic

Identify logic that can NEVER belong to Publisher.

## Research 8 — Publisher Skeleton

Construct the first Publisher skeleton containing ONLY responsibilities extracted from Telegram.

## Research 9 — Telegram Remainder

After Publisher extraction, describe what remains inside Telegram.

## Research 10 — Audit

Verify: nothing extracted twice, nothing lost, every operation classified, every responsibility traceable.
