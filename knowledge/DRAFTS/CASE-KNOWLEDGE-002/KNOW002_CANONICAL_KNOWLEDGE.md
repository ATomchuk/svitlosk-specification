# 2. Canonical Knowledge Candidates

## 2.1 Core Concepts

### CK-001: Publisher

**Statement:** Publisher is the logical Role responsible for preparing and distributing Publications.

**Evidence:** GLOSSARY §3, CASE-PUB-001, CASE-PUB-002, CASE-PUB-003, CASE-PUB-004, CASE-PUB-005

**Confidence:** HIGH

---

### CK-002: Lifecycle

**Statement:** Lifecycle is a pattern describing how information flows through the system, not an owned component.

**Evidence:** CASE-LC-001, CASE-PUB-004, CASE-PUB-005

**Confidence:** HIGH

---

### CK-003: Information Product

**Statement:** Information Product is a discrete unit of information produced by SvitloSk.

**Evidence:** CASE-INFPROD-001, CASE-TG-CORE-001

**Confidence:** HIGH

---

### CK-004: Publication

**Statement:** Publication is a public information message generated by SvitloSk for distribution.

**Evidence:** GLOSSARY §3, CASE-PUB-001, CASE-PUB-002

**Confidence:** HIGH

---

### CK-005: Journal Edition

**Statement:** Journal Edition is one daily instance of the Journal containing all Publications.

**Evidence:** CASE-JRN-001, CASE-INFPROD-001

**Confidence:** HIGH

---

## 2.2 Domain Concepts

### CK-006: Three Independent Domains

**Statement:** SvitloSk operates with three independent information domains: Queue Schedule, Planned Outages, Emergency Outages.

**Evidence:** CASE-SVT-ONTOLOGY-001, CASE-INFPROD-001, CASE-TG-CORE-001

**Confidence:** HIGH

**Confidence:** HIGH

### CK-008: Planned Outage

**Confidence:** HIGH

---

### CK-009: Emergency Outage

**Confidence:** HIGH
