# DRAFT-P-001_Research_Before_Change

**Document ID:** DRAFT-P-001

**Title:** Research Before Change

**Status:** Draft

---

# Statement

Always investigate before making architectural decisions.

---

# Reasoning

This principle emerged from the investigation methodology itself. Every investigation followed this principle:

1. CASE-001 investigated Publication before any conclusions
2. CASE-002A investigated Issue before any conclusions
3. META-001 investigated ontological types before any conclusions
4. No architectural decisions were made during research

The principle ensures that decisions are evidence-based, not assumption-based.

---

# Evidence

- All investigations followed this principle
- No architectural decisions were made during research
- All findings are evidence for future decisions
- Investigation artifacts contain only evidence, not decisions

---

# Source

Investigation methodology — emerged from practice, not design.

---

# Applications

- Every future architectural investigation should follow this principle
- Research should precede all design decisions
- Evidence should be collected before conclusions are drawn
