# Canonical Terminology

## Publication

**Canonical Term:** Publication

**Definition:** Information Object created for Communication.

**Synonyms (NOT canonical):**

- Message (forbidden in TJS-000A §17)
- Post (forbidden in TJS-000A §17)
- Telegram Message (different concept)

**Related Terms:**

- Publication Package (collection of Publications)
- Publication Channel (medium for distribution)
- Publication Engine (component that creates Publications)

---

## Issue

**Canonical Term:** Issue

**Definition:** Derivative of Publication, hybrid concept representing one editorial edition for a calendar day.

**Synonyms (NOT canonical):**

- Daily edition (informal)
- Journal edition (informal)

**Related Terms:**

- Journal (contains Issues)
- Publication (Issue depends on)

---

## Identity

**Canonical Term:** Identity

**Definition:** WHAT an object IS (intrinsic property).

**Synonyms (NOT canonical):**

- Identifier (different concept)
- ID (informal)
- Key (informal)

**Related Terms:**

- Identifier (HOW object is referred to)

---

## Identifier

**Canonical Term:** Identifier

**Definition:** HOW an object is referred to (assigned label).

**Synonyms (NOT canonical):**

- Identity (different concept)
- ID (informal)
- Key (informal)

**Related Terms:**

- Identity (WHAT object IS)

---

## Information

**Canonical Term:** Information

**Definition:** Relation between Reality and Knower.

**Synonyms (NOT canonical):**

- Data (different concept)
- Knowledge (different concept)
- Meaning (different concept)

**Related Terms:**

- Reality (what Information is about)
- Knower (who Information requires)

---

## Reality

**Canonical Term:** Reality

**Definition:** Physical existence independent of observers.

**Synonyms (NOT canonical):**

- World (informal)
- Physical world (informal)

**Related Terms:**

- Information (describes Reality)

---

## Representation

**Canonical Term:** Representation

**Definition:** Portrayal of Information in a format.

**Synonyms (NOT canonical):**

- Expression (informal)
- Presentation (informal)

**Related Terms:**

- Information (what Representation portrays)
- Carrier (medium that holds Representation)

---

## Communication

**Canonical Term:** Communication

**Definition:** Process of transferring meaning.

**Synonyms (NOT canonical):**

- Transfer (informal)
- Distribution (informal)

**Related Terms:**

- Information (what Communication transfers)
- Carrier (medium used for Communication)
