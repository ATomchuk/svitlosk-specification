# Minimal Research Kernel

## Irreducible Research Objects

| # | Object | Why Irreducible |
|---|--------|-----------------|
| 1 | Question | Research cannot begin without a question |
| 2 | Investigation | Research requires systematic inquiry |
| 3 | Evidence | Conclusions require proof |
| 4 | Finding | Research must produce conclusions |
| 5 | Knowledge | Findings must become permanent knowledge |

---

## Irreducible Research Processes

| # | Process | Why Irreducible |
|---|---------|-----------------|
| 1 | Evidence Collection | Cannot conclude without evidence |
| 2 | Knowledge Extraction | Findings must become Knowledge |
| 3 | Verification | Knowledge must be verified |

---

## Irreducible Research States

| # | State | Why Irreducible |
|---|-------|-----------------|
| 1 | UNKNOWN | Starting point |
| 2 | INVESTIGATED | Research has occurred |
| 3 | FOUND | Conclusions exist |
| 4 | VERIFIED | Conclusions are reliable |
| 5 | LOCKED | Conclusions are permanent |

# What Can Be Removed?

| Object | Removable? | Consequence |
|--------|------------|-------------|
| Models | YES | Research still works without models |
| Contradictions | YES | Research still works without contradiction search |
| Principles | YES | Research still works without principles |
| Audit | YES | Research still works without audit (but less reliable) |
| Cross-Examination | YES | Research still works without cross-examination (but less reliable) |

---

# What Cannot Be Removed?

| Object | Removable? | Consequence |
|--------|------------|-------------|
| Question | NO | Research cannot begin |
| Investigation | NO | Research cannot occur |
| Evidence | NO | Conclusions have no support |
| Finding | NO | Research has no output |
| Knowledge | NO | Findings are not preserved |

---

# Key Insight

**The minimal research kernel contains 5 objects, 3 processes, and 5 states.**

**This is the irreducible core of architectural research.**

**Any research process must include these elements to produce verified knowledge.**
