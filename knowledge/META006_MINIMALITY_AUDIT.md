# Minimality Audit

## Attempt 1: Remove "Question"

**Can research exist without a Question?**

**Argument FOR removal:**

- Some research is exploratory, not question-driven
- Some research starts with observation, not question
- Question may be implicit

**Argument AGAINST removal:**

- META-005's methodology explicitly starts with "Define research question"
- All CASE investigations began with a question
- Without a question, there is no direction

**Verdict:** Question CANNOT be removed. Minimal Kernel survives.

---

## Attempt 2: Remove "Investigation"

**Can research exist without Investigation?**

**Argument FOR removal:**

- Some knowledge is gained through observation, not investigation
- Investigation implies formal process; some research is informal

**Argument AGAINST removal:**

- META-005 defines research as "systematic inquiry"
- Investigation IS systematic inquiry
- Without Investigation, there is no research

**Verdict:** Investigation CANNOT be removed. Minimal Kernel survives.

---

## Attempt 3: Remove "Evidence"

**Can research exist without Evidence?**

**Argument FOR removal:**

- Some knowledge is intuitive or axiomatic
- Some conclusions are self-evident
- Evidence requirement may be too strict

**Argument AGAINST removal:**

- META-005's Invariant 1: "No Knowledge Without Evidence"
- All CASE investigations collected evidence
- Without Evidence, conclusions have no support

**Verdict:** Evidence CANNOT be removed. Minimal Kernel survives.

---

## Attempt 4: Remove "Finding"

- Without Findings, research has no output

**Verdict:** Finding CANNOT be removed. Minimal Kernel survives.
