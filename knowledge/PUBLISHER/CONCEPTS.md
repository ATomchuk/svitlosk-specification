# Purpose

---

# Core Concepts

## Publisher

**Definition:** The logical Role responsible for preparing and distributing Publications.

**Characteristics:**
- Publisher is an EXECUTOR, not a DECISION MAKER
- Publisher executes decisions made by Lifecycle, Parser, and Adapter
- Publisher does NOT own business decisions

**Evidence:** GLOSSARY §3, CASE-PUB-002, CASE-PUB-004, CASE-PUB-005

---

## Publication

**Definition:** A public information message generated by the SvitloSk system for distribution through one or more supported communication channels.

**Characteristics:**
- Publication is the atomic unit of information
- Publication is channel-independent
- Publication NEVER modifies official information

**Evidence:** GLOSSARY §3, CASE-PUB-001, CASE-PUB-002, CASE-INFPROD-001

---

## Information Product

**Definition:** A discrete unit of information produced by SvitloSk.

**Characteristics:**
- Information Product may be atomic or composite
- Information Product has identity, lifetime, and owner
- Information Product is the unit of publishing

**Evidence:** CASE-INFPROD-001, CASE-TG-CORE-001

---

## Journal Edition

**Definition:** One daily instance of the Journal containing all Publications.

**Characteristics:**
- Journal Edition is the composition root
- Journal Edition contains Publications organized by Territory
- Journal Edition exists for one Reporting Period

**Evidence:** CASE-JRN-001, CASE-INFPROD-001

---

## Journal

**Definition:** The ongoing public information service that continuously publishes information about electricity supply.

**Characteristics:**
- Journal is a SERVICE, not an object
- Journal is instantiated as Journal Editions
- Journal is channel-independent

**Evidence:** CHARTER §10, CASE-JRN-001, CASE-INFPROD-001

---

## Lifecycle

**Definition:** A pattern describing how information flows through the system.

**Characteristics:**
- Lifecycle is a PATTERN, not an owned component
- Lifecycle cannot be "owned" by any single component
- Lifecycle is OBSERVED across the entire system

**Evidence:** CASE-LC-001, CASE-PUB-004

---

## Representation

**Definition:** A channel-specific output of information.

**Characteristics:**
- Representation is channel-dependent
- Same information may have different Representations for different channels
- Representation is the final output to residents

**Evidence:** CASE-INF-001, CASE-TG-CORE-001, CASE-INFPROD-001

---

## Information

**Definition:** Factual content from official sources.

**Characteristics:**
- Information comes from DSO (Distribution System Operator)
- Information is NOT created by SvitloSk
- Information is INTERPRETED, not modified

**Evidence:** CASE-INF-001, CHARTER §7, §8

---

## Knowledge

**Definition:** Interpreted information that improves understanding.

**Characteristics:**
- Knowledge is CREATED by interpretation
- Knowledge does NOT change factual meaning
- Knowledge is derived from Information

**Evidence:** CASE-INF-001, GLOSSARY §2

---

## Parser

**Definition:** The component responsible for retrieving Open Data from approved Data Sources.

**Characteristics:**
- Parser is the ONLY component authorized to transform external data
- Parser preserves data integrity
- Parser does NOT interpret information

**Evidence:** GLOSSARY §2, CASE-SVT-ONTOLOGY-001

---

## Adapter

**Definition:** A channel-specific implementation of publishing.

**Characteristics:**
- Adapter handles channel-specific concerns
- Adapter is a PEER to other Adapters
- Adapter does NOT own publication logic

**Evidence:** CASE-SVT-ONTOLOGY-001, CASE-TG-CORE-001

---

## Territory

**Definition:** A geographic unit for publication organization.

**Characteristics:**
- Territory organizes Publications geographically
- Territory follows the Territorial Model hierarchy
- Territory is a reference entity

**Evidence:** GLOSSARY §4, CASE-INFPROD-001, CASE-TG-CORE-001
