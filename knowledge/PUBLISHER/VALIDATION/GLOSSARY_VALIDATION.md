### Publication

**Status:** IMPLEMENTED

**Evidence:**
- GLOSSARY.md §3: "Publication: A public information message generated by the SvitloSk system."
- TELEGRAM_PUBLICATION_LIFECYCLE.md defines publication states.

**Confidence:** HIGH

### Information Product

**Status:** INFERRED

**Evidence:**
- No explicit "Information Product" in Telegram specifications.
- Concept inferred from CASE-INFPROD-001.

**Reason:** Telegram uses "Publication" not "Information Product."

**Confidence:** MEDIUM

### Journal Edition

**Status:** INFERRED

**Evidence:**
- TELEGRAM_PUBLICATION_LIFECYCLE.md §8: "An Issue opens when the first Publication for a calendar day is generated."
- "Journal Edition" is a generalization of "Issue."

**Reason:** Telegram uses "Issue," not "Journal Edition."

**Confidence:** MEDIUM
