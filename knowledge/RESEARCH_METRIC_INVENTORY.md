# Existing Metrics

## Current Metric: Overall Research Coverage = 45%

**Source:** META-003

**Definition:** The percentage of repository concepts that have been investigated.

**Calculation Method:**

```
Overall Coverage = (Investigated Concepts / Total Concepts) × 100
```

**Data:**

- Total Concepts: 28
- Investigated Concepts: 13 (DEEPLY + PARTIALLY)
- Coverage: (13/28) × 100 = 46.4% ≈ 45%

**Limitations:**

- Does not account for depth of investigation
- Does not account for relationship coverage
- Does not account for document coverage
- Does not account for knowledge unit coverage
- Not reproducible without same concept extraction method
