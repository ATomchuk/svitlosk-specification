# Final Answers

## 1. What exactly does "45% investigated" mean?

**It means that 45% of repository concepts have been investigated according to the Concept Coverage metric.**

**It does NOT mean:**

- 45% of relationships are investigated
- 45% of documents are audited
- 45% of knowledge is extracted
- 45% of the repository is understood

---

## 2. Can that number be reproduced independently?

**YES, but only with the same:**

- Concept extraction method
- Maturity level definitions
- Coverage formula

**Without these, the number is NOT reproducible.**

---

## 3. Is it mathematically valid?

**YES, as a single-dimension metric.**

**But it is INCOMPLETE as a measure of research coverage.**

---

## 4. Can research coverage be objectively measured?

**YES, using a multi-dimensional metric like RCI.**

**RCI requires:**

- Fixed weights for each component
- Fixed formulas for each component
- Agreement on what "coverage" means
