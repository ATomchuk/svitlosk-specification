- Analyze contradictions
- Extract findings

- Evidence-based

**Weaknesses:**

- No formal methodology
- Ad-hoc process

**Methodology:**

# Architectural Drift Analysis

| Phase | Drift | Evidence |
|-------|-------|----------|
| CASE-001 → META-001 | Ontology disconnected from process | META-001 did not connect to research |
| META-001 → K-001 | Focus shifted to objects | K-001 focused on knowledge objects, not operations |
| K-001 → META-003 | Focus shifted to measurement | META-003 measured coverage, not root causes |
| META-003 → META-005 | Focus shifted to architecture | META-005 modeled architecture, not cognitive operations |
| META-005 → META-006 | Falsification revealed gaps | META-006 found 12 missing objects |

---

# Key Drift Point

**The critical drift occurred between META-001 and META-005.**

**META-001 identified ontological types but did not connect them to research process.**

**META-005 modeled research architecture but missed CONTEXT, SCOPE, and EPISTEMOLOGICAL dimensions.**

**The drift was: FROM ontology TO structure, missing the OPERATIONAL layer.**

---

# Key Insight

**Research evolved from ontology (META-001) to structure (META-005) without modeling OPERATIONS.**

**The missing layer is COGNITIVE OPERATIONS — the intellectual activities that produce knowledge.**

**META-005 modeled what EXISTS in research, but not what IS DONE in research.**
