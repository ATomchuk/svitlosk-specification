# Invariant Falsification

## Invariant 1: No Knowledge Without Evidence

**Counterexample attempt:** Can knowledge exist without evidence?

**Evidence found:**

- Logical axioms (e.g., "A = A") require no evidence
- Mathematical truths require no empirical evidence
- Definitional knowledge requires no external evidence

**Verdict:** PARTIALLY FALSIFIED — Invariant 1 applies to EMPIRICAL knowledge, not AXIOMATIC knowledge.

---

## Invariant 2: No Specification Before Verified Knowledge

**Counterexample attempt:** Can specification exist before verification?

**Evidence found:**

- META-005 itself admits: "Status: VIOLATED in some cases"
- Some specifications were created before investigations
- Some specifications contained assumptions

**Verdict:** FALSIFIED — Invariant 2 was violated in practice.
