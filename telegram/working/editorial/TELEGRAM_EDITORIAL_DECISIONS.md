# Editorial Decisions

## D-001: Reader Priority Decision

**Decision:** All editorial decisions SHALL prioritize the informational needs of the Starokostiantyniv Community.

**Source:** TJS-001 §8

**Type:** Audience priority

---

## D-002: Accuracy Priority Decision

**Decision:** Publishing accurate information is mandatory. Accuracy SHALL take priority over speed.

**Source:** PP P-04

**Type:** Quality priority

---

## D-003: Source Fidelity Decision

**Decision:** Every publication must increase clarity without changing the meaning of the official source. The journal does not interpret, predict or alter official information.

**Source:** TJS-001 §6

**Type:** Editorial integrity

---

## D-004: Territory-First Decision

**Decision:** Territory name is always the first element of the publication. A post represents only one territory.

**Source:** TJS-004 §Territory

**Type:** Structure decision

---

## D-005: Category Ordering Decision

**Decision:** Inside every post information is presented in the following order: 1. Planned outages. 2. Emergency outages. If one category has no records, it is omitted.

**Source:** TJS-004 §Categories

**Type:** Structure decision

---

## D-006: Empty Post Decision

**Decision:** Posts without information are not created.

**Source:** TJS-004 §Package

**Type:** Creation decision

---

## D-007: Formatting Limitation Decision

**Decision:** Only bold and plain text are permitted. Italic, underline, spoiler, blockquote, code blocks and other formatting are prohibited.

**Source:** TJS-004 §Formatting

**Type:** Formatting decision

---

## D-008: Branding Decision

**Decision:** Posts do not contain signatures. Posts do not contain decorative separators. Posts do not contain location icons. The Telegram channel itself represents the SvitloSk identity.

**Source:** TJS-004 §Branding

**Type:** Identity decision

---

## D-009: Automatic Generation Decision

**Decision:** Every publication is generated automatically from structured data. Manual editing is not part of the standard publication workflow.

**Source:** TJS-004 §Automatic

**Type:** Workflow decision

---

## D-010: Update Meaning Preservation Decision

**Decision:** Every update shall preserve the original meaning of the official information.

**Source:** TJS-002 §8

**Type:** Integrity decision

---

## D-011: Editing Permissibility Decision

**Decision:** After publication: factual corrections are permitted; layout corrections are permitted; the meaning of official information shall never change.

**Source:** TJS-003 §10

**Type:** Editing decision

---

## D-012: Event-Driven Update Decision

**Decision:** Publication updates SHALL occur only after a new normalized Dataset becomes available. Time alone SHALL NOT trigger Publication updates.

**Source:** TJS-008 §7

**Type:** Synchronization decision

---

## D-013: In-Place Update Decision

**Decision:** Existing Publications SHALL be updated in place whenever possible. Publications SHALL NOT be deleted and recreated solely because their content has changed.

**Source:** TJS-008 §8

**Type:** Lifecycle decision

---

## D-014: Deletion Timing Decision

**Decision:** Deletion SHALL occur only when the lifecycle of a Publication has ended.

**Source:** TJS-008 §8

**Type:** Lifecycle decision

---

## D-015: Daily Schedule Permanence Decision

**Decision:** The daily schedule shall never be deleted.

**Source:** TJS-002 §5

**Type:** Preservation decision

---

## D-016: Emergency Retention Decision

**Decision:** Emergency publications shall remain available as part of the public historical record.

**Source:** TJS-002 §6

**Type:** Preservation decision

---

## D-017: Tomorrow Package Separation Decision

**Decision:** Tomorrow Publications SHALL form a separate Publication Package. Tomorrow Publications SHALL be temporary.

**Source:** TJS-008 §5

**Type:** Structure decision

---

## D-018: Compliance Decision

**Decision:** Every publication generated by SvitloSk shall comply with this editorial policy.

**Source:** TJS-004 §Compliance

**Type:** Governance decision

---

## D-019: Terminology Consistency Decision

**Decision:** The project uses the term "Outage". The terminology must remain consistent throughout every publication.

**Source:** TJS-004 §Terminology

**Type:** Terminology decision

---

## D-020: Update Timestamp Decision

**Decision:** Each publication ends with the update timestamp.

**Source:** TJS-004 §Update Information

**Type:** Structure decision
