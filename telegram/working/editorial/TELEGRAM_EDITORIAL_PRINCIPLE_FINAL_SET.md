# Purpose

This document defines the final canonical set of Editorial Principles for the Telegram Editorial System. This set is frozen and SHALL NOT change without formal governance.

---

## EP-001: Reader First Principle

**Ukrainian:** Пріоритет читача

**Canonical Definition:** The primary audience is the residents of the Starokostiantyniv Territorial Community. All editorial decisions SHALL prioritize the informational needs of this community.

**Justification:** Core audience principle that ensures every publication serves the community. Directly supports the SvitloSk mission of providing information to residents.

---

## EP-002: Editorial Truth Principle

**Ukrainian:** Редакційна правда

**Canonical Definition:** Every publication SHALL increase clarity without changing the meaning of the official source. The Journal does not interpret, predict or alter official information. No assumptions, forecasts or predictions SHALL be presented as facts.

**Justification:** Core integrity principle that ensures faithful representation of official data. Absorbs the former "Source Fidelity" principle.

---

## EP-003: Editorial Silence Principle

**Ukrainian:** Редакційна тиша

**Canonical Definition:** SvitloSk does not modify open data. SvitloSk does not create new facts. SvitloSk does not produce forecasts. SvitloSk does not alter the meaning of officially published information.

**Justification:** Defines editorial restraint — what the Journal does NOT do. Distinct from "Truth" (what IS said) and "Fidelity" (how it's said).

---

## EP-004: Minimal Editorial Intervention Principle

**Ukrainian:** Мінімальне редакційне втручання

**Canonical Definition:** Every publication SHALL be generated automatically from structured data. Manual editing is not part of the standard publication workflow. After publication: factual corrections are permitted; layout corrections are permitted; the meaning of official information SHALL never change.

**Justification:** Defines the boundary between automatic and manual editing. Supports automation and consistency.

---

## EP-005: Issue Integrity Principle

**Ukrainian:** Цілісність випуску

**Canonical Definition:** The Telegram Journal serves as the permanent public archive of publications related to the current day. Daily publications concerning planned and emergency outages SHALL remain available as part of the historical record.

**Justification:** Ensures historical preservation and accountability. Supports the Journal's role as a public archive.

---

## EP-007: Deterministic Editorial Behaviour Principle

**Ukrainian:** Детермінована редакційна поведінка

**Canonical Definition:** Two identical normalized Datasets SHALL always generate identical Telegram Journals. Publication order, formatting and content SHALL remain deterministic.

**Justification:** Core quality principle that ensures reproducibility and reliability. Supports trust in the Journal.

---

## EP-009: Territorial Organization Principle

**Ukrainian:** Принцип територіальної організації

**Canonical Definition:** Information SHALL always be organized according to the territorial structure of the community.

**Justification:** Defines how information is structured to match the community's geography. Ensures readers can find information relevant to their location. Replaces the narrower "Territory-First" principle.

---

## EP-010: Consistency Principle

**Ukrainian:** Послідовність

**Canonical Definition:** Every publication SHALL be consistent, easy to read, predictable and automatically generated from structured data.

**Justification:** Supports quality and predictability. Ensures readers know what to expect from every publication.

---

## EP-013: Accessibility Principle

**Ukrainian:** Доступність

**Canonical Definition:** Publications SHALL be accessible to all residents regardless of technical experience or device.

**Justification:** Supports the social mission of equal access. Ensures publications are usable by everyone in the community.

---

## EP-014: Timeliness Principle

**Ukrainian:** Своєчасність

**Canonical Definition:** Publications SHALL be delivered promptly when official data becomes available.

**Justification:** Supports the mission of timely information delivery. Ensures residents receive outage information quickly.
