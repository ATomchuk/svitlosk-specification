# Final Output

## 1. What is Reality?

**Reality is physical existence — what exists independently of humans, systems, and information.**

**Reality does not change because of Information. Information describes Reality.**

---

## 2. What is Fact?

**Fact is a CLAIM about Reality made by a claimant.**

**Fact is NOT Reality itself — it is a statement about Reality.**

**Fact can be true or false, can disappear, can be contradicted.**

---

## 3. What is Knowledge?

**Knowledge is BELIEF held by a Knower about Reality.**

**Knowledge requires a Knower to exist.**

**Knowledge can be true or false, can change, can be forgotten.**

---

## 4. What is Information?

**Information is a RELATION between Reality and a Knower, or a DIFFERENCE that makes a difference.**

**Information requires BOTH Reality (subject) and a Knower (observer).**

**Information is NOT a thing that exists independently.**

---

## 5. What is Representation?

**Representation is the FORM of Information — a portrayal in a specific format or medium.**

**Representation CONTAINS Information.**

**Multiple Representations can describe same Information.**

---

## 6. What is Carrier?

**Carrier is the MEDIUM that holds Representation — the physical or digital vessel.**

**Carrier is NOT Information — it is the container.**

**Carrier CAN change while Information remains identical.**

---

## 7. Where exactly does Publication first appear?

**Publication appears at the INFORMATION layer.**

**Publication is Information structured for distribution — it is the point where Knowledge becomes communicable.**

---

## 8. Which semantic chain best explains SvitloSk?

**The chain: Reality → Fact → Knowledge → Information → Representation → Carrier**

**This chain explains how a power outage (Reality) becomes a Telegram message (Carrier) that residents can understand.**
