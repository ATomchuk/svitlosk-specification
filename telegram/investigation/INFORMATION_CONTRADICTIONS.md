# Contradictions Found

## Contradiction 1: Information Creation vs Disclosure

**Statement A:** Information is created by Observation

**Statement B:** Official Publication discloses Information

**Contradiction:** Does Information exist before Official Publication?

**Evidence:**

- Observation creates Knowledge → Knowledge becomes Information
- Official Publication makes Information public
- Information exists BEFORE Official Publication (as private knowledge)

**Resolution:** Information is CREATED by Observation, DISCLOSED by Official Publication.

**Severity:** LOW

---

## Contradiction 2: Information vs Open Data

**Statement A:** "Open Data — Officially published information"

**Statement B:** "Information" is used more broadly in GLOSSARY.md

**Contradiction:** Is Open Data the same as Information?

**Evidence:**

- Open Data is INFORMATION that is officially published
- Information can be private or public
- Open Data is a SUBSET of Information

**Resolution:** Open Data IS Information, but not all Information is Open Data.

**Severity:** LOW

---

## Contradiction 3: Information vs Publication

**Statement A:** "Publication — A public information message"

**Statement B:** "Interpretation Result — The normalized information"

**Contradiction:** Is Publication the same as Information?

**Evidence:**

- Publication PACKAGES Information
- Publication is a MESSAGE containing Information
- Publication is not Information itself

**Resolution:** Publication is a CONTAINER for Information, not Information itself.

**Severity:** MEDIUM

---

## Contradiction 4: Information vs Knowledge

**Statement A:** Information requires Knowledge

**Statement B:** Knowledge requires Observation

**Contradiction:** What comes first — Information or Knowledge?

**Evidence:**

- Observation → Knowledge → Information
- Knowledge is understanding; Information is structured for communication
- Knowledge comes BEFORE Information

**Resolution:** Knowledge is precursor to Information.

**Severity:** LOW

---

## Contradiction 5: Information vs Reality

**Statement A:** Information describes Reality

**Statement B:** Reality exists independently of Information

**Contradiction:** Can Information exist without Reality?

**Evidence:**

- Information requires a subject (Reality)
- Without Reality, there is nothing to be informed about
- Information CANNOT exist without Reality

**Resolution:** Information DEPENDS on Reality for its subject.

**Severity:** LOW

---

## Contradiction 6: Information vs Interpretation

**Statement A:** "Interpretation — transforming structured information into information understandable"

**Statement B:** "Interpretation Result — normalized information"

**Contradiction:** Does Interpretation create new Information?

**Evidence:**

- GLOSSARY.md: "Interpretation SHALL improve readability without changing factual meaning"
- Interpretation does NOT create new facts
- Interpretation REORGANIZES existing Information

**Resolution:** Interpretation REORGANIZES, not creates.

**Severity:** LOW

---

## Contradiction 7: Information vs Publication (Dual Meaning)

**Statement A:** "Publication" means "public information message"

**Statement B:** "Publication" means "act of publishing"

**Contradiction:** Is Publication a thing or an action?

**Evidence:**

- GLOSSARY.md defines Publication as "message" (thing)
- But "publication" can also mean "act of publishing" (action)
- Context determines meaning

**Resolution:** In SvitloSk, Publication is a THING (message), not an action.

**Severity:** MEDIUM
