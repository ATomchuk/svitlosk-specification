# Publication Boundary Investigation

## Is Publication Boundary Between Information and Representation?

**Answer:** YES

**Evidence:**

- Publication is Information structured for distribution
- Publication is rendered as Representation
- Publication bridges Information and Representation

**Conclusion:** Publication IS the boundary between Information and Representation.

---

## Is Publication Boundary Between Information and Communication?

**Answer:** PARTIALLY

**Evidence:**

- Publication is Information structured for distribution
- Publication is INPUT to Communication
- Publication bridges Information and Communication

**Conclusion:** Publication is PARTIALLY the boundary between Information and Communication.

---

## Is Publication Boundary Between Communication and Delivery?

**Answer:** NO

**Evidence:**

- Communication is the process
- Delivery is the mechanism
- Publication is the content, not the boundary

**Conclusion:** Publication is NOT the boundary between Communication and Delivery.

---

## Is Publication Boundary Between Editorial and Technical Worlds?

**Answer:** PARTIALLY

**Evidence:**

- Publication follows Editorial Policy (editorial)
- Publication is generated by Publication Engine (technical)
- Publication bridges editorial and technical

**Conclusion:** Publication is PARTIALLY the boundary between editorial and technical.

---

# Publication Boundary Summary

| Boundary | Publication Is Boundary? | Evidence |
|----------|-------------------------|----------|
| Information ↔ Representation | YES | Publication bridges content and form |
| Information ↔ Communication | PARTIALLY | Publication bridges content and process |
| Communication ↔ Delivery | NO | Publication is content, not boundary |
| Editorial ↔ Technical | PARTIALLY | Publication bridges policy and implementation |

---

# Key Insight

**Publication is primarily the BOUNDARY between Information and Representation.**

**Publication bridges content (Information) and form (Representation).**

**Publication is the point where Information becomes representable.**
