# Purpose

# Contradictions Found

## Contradiction 1: Publication as Information vs Communication Object

**Statement A:** Publication is Information structured for distribution

**Statement B:** Publication is an object created for Communication purpose

**Contradiction:** Is Publication Information or Communication Object?

**Evidence:**

- Publication contains Information (content)
- Publication is created for Communication (purpose)
- These are different aspects

**Resolution:** Publication is Information Object CREATED FOR Communication. Both are true.

**Severity:** LOW

---

## Contradiction 2: Publication vs Communication

**Statement A:** Publication is INPUT to Communication

**Statement B:** Publication IS Communication

**Contradiction:** Is Publication part of Communication or separate from it?

**Evidence:**

- Publication exists BEFORE Communication begins
- Communication is the PROCESS; Publication is the CONTENT
- They are different layers

**Resolution:** Publication is INPUT to Communication, not Communication itself.

**Severity:** LOW

---

## Contradiction 3: Publication Identity vs Information Identity

**Statement A:** Publication creates its own identity (Territory + Date + Template)

**Statement B:** Publication is derived from Information

**Contradiction:** Does Publication inherit or create identity?

**Evidence:**

- Information has no identity (Information is relationship)
- Publication creates identity independently
- Publication identity is unique

**Resolution:** Publication CREATES its own identity, does NOT inherit from Information.

**Severity:** LOW

---

## Contradiction 4: Publication Position

**Statement A:** Publication appears AFTER Information

**Statement B:** Publication appears BEFORE Representation

**Contradiction:** Where exactly does Publication appear?

**Evidence:**

- Publication is created from Interpretation Result (Information)
- Publication is rendered as Representation
- Publication is BETWEEN Information and Representation

**Resolution:** Publication appears BETWEEN Information and Representation.

**Severity:** LOW

---

## Contradiction 5: Publication Purpose

**Statement A:** Publication exists for distribution

**Statement B:** Publication exists for Communication

**Contradiction:** Is Publication purpose distribution or Communication?

**Evidence:**

- Distribution is mechanism; Communication is goal
- Publication serves both

**Resolution:** Publication serves Communication through distribution.

**Severity:** LOW
