# Purpose

# Open Data Investigation

## Evidence

| Source | Statement |
|--------|-----------|
| GLOSSARY.md §2 | "Open Data — Officially published information that is publicly accessible without authentication or special permission" |
| GLOSSARY.md §2 | "Open Data SHALL remain the authoritative source of factual information" |
| GLOSSARY.md §2 | "SvitloSk SHALL NOT modify the factual meaning of Open Data" |
| CHARTER.md §8 | "SvitloSk respects the integrity of open public information" |
| CHARTER.md §8 | "Official sources always remain authoritative" |

---

## Is Open Data Information?

**Answer:** YES

**Evidence:**

- GLOSSARY.md explicitly defines Open Data as "information"
- Open Data has all properties of Information
- Open Data is Information with public access

**Conclusion:** Open Data IS Information.

---

## Is Open Data a Representation?

**Answer:** PARTIALLY

**Evidence:**

- Open Data represents Reality for the public
- Open Data is not Reality itself
- Open Data can be accurate or inaccurate

**Conclusion:** Open Data IS a representation of Reality.

---

## Is Open Data a Container?

**Answer:** NO

**Evidence:**

- Open Data is the information itself
- Open Data is not a vessel containing information
- Open Data IS the content

**Conclusion:** Open Data is NOT a container.

---

## Is Open Data Evidence?

**Answer:** YES

**Evidence:**

- Open Data provides evidence of Reality
- Open Data can be used to verify facts
- Open Data is traceable to source

**Conclusion:** Open Data IS evidence of Reality.

---

## Is Open Data a Projection?

**Answer:** PARTIALLY

**Evidence:**

- Open Data projects Reality for public consumption
- Open Data is one representation of Reality
- Open Data may omit details

**Conclusion:** Open Data IS a projection of Reality.

**Open Data IS Information that is officially published and publicly accessible.**

**Open Data IS evidence of Reality.**

**Open Data IS a representation/projection of Reality.**

**Open Data IS NOT a container — it IS the content.**
