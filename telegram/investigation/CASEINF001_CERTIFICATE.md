# Final Output

## 1. What is Information?

**Information is a RELATION between Reality and a Knower, or a REPRESENTATION of Reality for a Knower.**

**Information requires BOTH Reality (subject) and a Knower (observer).**

**Information is NOT a thing that exists independently — it exists in the RELATIONSHIP between Reality and Knowledge.**

## 4. Is Open Data Information or Representation?

**BOTH.**

**Evidence:**

- Open Data IS Information (officially published information)
- Open Data IS a Representation (projects Reality for public)
- Open Data IS Evidence (provides evidence of Reality)

## 5. Which ontology best explains the SvitloSk domain?

**Model B (Information as Relation) and Model F (Information as Representation) best explain the SvitloSk domain.**

**Evidence:**

- Information requires Reality (subject) and Knower (observer) — Model B

- Both models together explain why Information exists, how it is created, and how it is used
