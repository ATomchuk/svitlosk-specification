# Contradictions Found

## Contradiction 1: Fact vs Knowledge

**Statement A:** Fact is a claim about Reality

**Statement B:** Knowledge is belief held by Knower

**Contradiction:** Are Fact and Knowledge the same?

**Evidence:**

- Fact is a CLAIM (external statement)
- Knowledge is a BELIEF (internal state)
- Different people can have different Knowledge about same Fact
- Fact can be true while Knowledge is wrong

**Resolution:** Fact and Knowledge are DIFFERENT. Fact is external claim; Knowledge is internal belief.

**Severity:** LOW

---

## Contradiction 2: Information vs Knowledge

**Statement A:** Information is relationship between Reality and Knower

**Statement B:** Knowledge is belief held by Knower

**Contradiction:** Are Information and Knowledge the same?

**Evidence:**

- Information is structured for communication
- Knowledge is held by Knower
- Information can exist without Knower knowing it (in documents)
- Knowledge requires Knower to hold it

**Resolution:** Information and Knowledge are DIFFERENT. Information is communicable; Knowledge is held.

**Severity:** LOW

---

## Contradiction 3: Information vs Representation

**Statement A:** Information is relationship between Reality and Knower

**Statement B:** Representation is portrayal of Information

**Contradiction:** Are Information and Representation the same?

**Evidence:**

- Information is the CONTENT
- Representation is the FORM
- Same Information can have multiple Representations
- Representation contains Information

**Resolution:** Information and Representation are DIFFERENT. Information is content; Representation is form.

**Severity:** LOW

---

## Contradiction 4: Representation vs Carrier

**Statement A:** Representation is portrayal of Information

**Statement B:** Carrier is medium holding Representation

**Contradiction:** Are Representation and Carrier the same?

**Evidence:**

- Representation is the FORM of Information
- Carrier is the MEDIUM holding Representation
- Same Representation can use different Carriers
- Carrier can change while Representation remains identical

**Resolution:** Representation and Carrier are DIFFERENT. Representation is form; Carrier is medium.

**Severity:** LOW

---

## Contradiction 5: Fact vs Information

**Statement A:** Fact is claim about Reality

**Statement B:** Information is relationship between Reality and Knower

**Contradiction:** Are Fact and Information the same?

**Evidence:**

- Fact is a CLAIM (external statement)
- Information is a RELATIONSHIP (connection)
- Fact can be false; Information is about relationship
- Different people can have different Information about same Fact

**Resolution:** Fact and Information are DIFFERENT. Fact is claim; Information is relationship.

**Severity:** LOW

---

## Contradiction 6: Knowledge vs Information Order

**Statement A:** Knowledge comes BEFORE Information

**Statement B:** Information is needed to form Knowledge

**Contradiction:** Which comes first — Knowledge or Information?

**Evidence:**

- Observation produces Knowledge
- Knowledge is structured as Information
- But Information requires Knowledge to exist
- Circular dependency?

**Resolution:** Knowledge comes FIRST (from Observation), then is structured as Information. No circular dependency.

**Severity:** MEDIUM

---

## Contradiction 7: Publication Position

**Statement A:** Publication is Information structured for distribution

**Statement B:** Publication is Representation of Information

**Contradiction:** Is Publication Information or Representation?

**Evidence:**

- Publication contains Information (content)
- Publication is formatted for delivery (form)
- Publication is BOTH Information and Representation

**Resolution:** Publication is a HYBRID — it is Information packaged as Representation.

**Severity:** MEDIUM
