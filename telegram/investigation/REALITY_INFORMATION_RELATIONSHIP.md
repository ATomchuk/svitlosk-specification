## Analysis

### Does Reality Create Information?

**Answer:** NO

**Evidence:**

- Reality exists independently of Information
- Reality does not "create" Information
- Information requires Observation to exist

**Conclusion:** Reality does NOT create Information. Observation creates Information about Reality.

---

### Does Information Describe Reality?

**Answer:** YES

**Evidence:**

- Information is about Reality
- Information represents Reality
- Information describes Reality's properties

**Conclusion:** Information DESCRIBES Reality.

---

### Can Information Be Wrong About Reality?

**Answer:** YES

**Evidence:**

- Information can be inaccurate
- Information can be outdated
- Information can be misinterpreted

**Conclusion:** Information CAN be wrong about Reality.

---

### Does Reality Change Based on Information?

**Answer:** NO

**Evidence:**

- Reality exists independently
- Information does not change Reality
- Reality changes based on physical causes, not information

**Conclusion:** Reality does NOT change based on Information.

# Key Insight

**Reality and Information have a DESCRIBING relationship, not a CREATING relationship.**

**Reality provides the SUBJECT for Information.**

**Information provides the DESCRIPTION of Reality.**

**Neither creates the other.**
