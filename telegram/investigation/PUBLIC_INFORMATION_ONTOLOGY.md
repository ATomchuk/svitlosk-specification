## Analysis

### Is Public Information an Object?

**Answer:** NO

**Evidence:**

- Public Information is a PROPERTY of officially published data
- Public Information describes the ACCESS LEVEL of information
- Public Information is not a thing that can be pointed to

**Classification:** PROPERTY

---

### Is Public Information a Property?

**Answer:** YES

**Evidence:**

- "Public" describes the access level of information
- Information can be public or private
- Public is an attribute, not an entity

**Classification:** PROPERTY

---

### Is Public Information a State?

**Answer:** NO

**Evidence:**

- State implies change over time
- Public Information is a persistent characteristic
- Public Information doesn't transition between states

**Classification:** NOT STATE

---

### Is Public Information a Relation?

**Answer:** NO

**Evidence:**

- Relation implies connection between two entities
- Public Information is a characteristic of one entity
- Public Information doesn't connect two things

**Classification:** NOT RELATION

---

### Is Public Information a Process?

**Answer:** NO

**Evidence:**

- Process implies action over time
- Public Information is a static characteristic
- Public Information doesn't perform actions

**Classification:** NOT PROCESS

---

### Is Public Information a Projection?

**Answer:** NO

**Evidence:**

- Projection implies representation of something else
- Public Information is the actual characteristic, not a representation
- Public Information is the real thing

**Classification:** NOT PROJECTION

---

## Can Public Information Exist Without Publication?

**Answer:** YES

**Evidence:**

- DSO publishes public information on its website
- Government publishes public information in registries
- Public information exists independently of SvitloSk

**Conclusion:** Public Information is NOT dependent on Publication.
