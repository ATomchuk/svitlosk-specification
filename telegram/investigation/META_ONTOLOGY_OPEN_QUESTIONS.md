# Open Questions

## Question 1: Are 8 Meta-Levels Sufficient?

**Question:** Are 8 meta-levels sufficient to describe any information system?

**Current State:** 8 levels identified; but may be reducible or expandable.

**Impact:** HIGH — affects meta-ontology completeness.

## Question 2: Can Levels Be Merged?

**Question:** Can any two meta-levels be merged without losing information?

**Current State:** Each level describes distinct aspect; merging would lose precision.

**Impact:** MEDIUM — affects meta-ontology parsimony.

**Question:** Can any meta-level be split into more specific levels?

**Impact:** MEDIUM — affects meta-ontology granularity.

**Question:** Do levels depend on each other, or are they independent?

## Question 5: Can a Concept Exist at Multiple Levels?

**Question:** Can a concept belong to multiple meta-levels simultaneously?

**Current State:** YES — Publication belongs to Ontological, Semantic, and Communicative levels.

**Impact:** MEDIUM — affects classification model.

## Question 6: What Is the Root Level?

**Question:** Is Reality the root, or is there something beneath Reality?

**Current State:** Reality is Level 0 (root). Nothing beneath it.

**Impact:** LOW — affects meta-ontology foundation.

## Question 7: What Is the Terminal Level?

**Question:** Is Communication the terminal level, or is there something beyond?

**Current State:** Communication is Level 4 (terminal for information flow). But Structural, Social, Temporal levels exist beyond.

**Impact:** LOW — affects meta-ontology scope.
