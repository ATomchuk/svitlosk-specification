---

## Does Official Publication CREATE Information?

**Answer:** NO

**Evidence:**

- Information already exists (dispatcher knows about outage)
- Official Publication does not create new facts
- Official Publication only changes ACCESS LEVEL

**Conclusion:** Official Publication does NOT create Information.

---

## Does Official Publication DISCLOSE Information?

**Answer:** YES

**Evidence:**

- Official Publication makes private information public
- Official Publication changes access from private to public
- Official Publication reveals information to residents

**Conclusion:** Official Publication DISCLOSES Information.

---

## Does Official Publication CHANGE Information?

**Answer:** NO

**Evidence:**

- GLOSSARY.md: "SvitloSk SHALL NOT modify the factual meaning of Open Data"
- Official Publication preserves factual meaning
- Official Publication only changes ACCESS LEVEL

**Conclusion:** Official Publication does NOT change Information MEANING.

---

## Does Official Publication CHANGE ACCESS?

**Answer:** YES

**Evidence:**

- Before Official Publication: information is private (DSO knows)
- After Official Publication: information is public (residents can access)
- Access level changes from private to public

**Conclusion:** Official Publication CHANGES ACCESS LEVEL.

# Key Insight

**Official Publication is a DISCLOSURE mechanism, not a CREATION mechanism.**

**Information exists BEFORE Official Publication (as private knowledge).**

**Official Publication makes private Information PUBLIC.**

**Official Publication changes ACCESS, not MEANING.**
