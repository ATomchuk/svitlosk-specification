# Category Classification

## Ontological Categories (Layer 1)

| Category | Definition | Evidence |
|----------|------------|----------|
| Object | Thing with identity that persists over time | Models entities in world |
| Event | Something that happens at a point in time | Models occurrences |
| Process | Sequence of actions that transforms inputs | Models activities |
| State | Condition of an Object at a point in time | Models status |

## Epistemological Categories (Layer 2)

| Category | Definition | Evidence |
|----------|------------|----------|
| Knowledge | Belief held by a Knower about Reality | Models understanding |
| Identity | What makes an Object the same over time | Models uniqueness |
| Information | Relation between Reality and a Knower | Models knowledge content |

| Category | Definition | Evidence |
