# KF-007_Information_Requires_Knower

**Document ID:** KF-007

**Title:** Information Requires a Knower

**Document Class:** Knowledge Finding

**Status:** Validated

---

# Statement

Information cannot exist without a conscious observer (Knower). Information is a relation between Reality and a Knower, not an independent entity.

---

# Meaning

Information is not a thing that exists independently. Information requires both a subject (Reality) and an observer (Knower). Without a Knower, there is only Reality, not Information. This means Information is fundamentally relational, not substantial.
