# KF-003_Publication_Bridges_Information_Representation

**Document ID:** KF-003

**Title:** Publication Bridges Information and Representation

**Document Class:** Knowledge Finding

**Status:** Validated

---

# Statement

Publication is the boundary between Information (content) and Representation (form). Publication is Information structured for distribution.

---

# Meaning

Publication occupies a unique position in the semantic chain. It is neither pure Information (which has no identity or lifecycle) nor pure Representation (which is merely a portrayal). Publication is Information that has been given identity, lifecycle, and purpose for Communication.
