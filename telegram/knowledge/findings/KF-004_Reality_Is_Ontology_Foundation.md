# KF-004_Reality_Is_Ontology_Foundation

**Document ID:** KF-004

**Title:** Reality Is the Foundation of Ontology

**Document Class:** Knowledge Finding

**Status:** Validated

---

# Statement

Reality exists independently and is the foundation of the entire ontological chain. Everything else depends on Reality.

---

# Meaning

Reality is the physical world that exists independently of observers, systems, and information. All other ontological concepts (Object, Event, Process, Information, etc.) are descriptions of or relations to Reality. Without Reality, nothing else can exist.
