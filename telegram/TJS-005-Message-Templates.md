# TJS-005 — Message Templates

Status: Draft (Чернетка)

Specification ID: TJS-005

Project: SvitloSk

Class: Normative

---

# 1. Purpose

This specification defines the canonical publication templates used by the SvitloSk Telegram Journal.

It establishes the normative structure, hierarchy, formatting and validation rules for every Telegram Publication generated by the Publication Engine.

The objective is to ensure that every Publication remains deterministic, readable and structurally identical regardless of publication date or data volume.

---

# 2. Scope

This specification applies to every automatically generated Telegram Publication, including:

- City Today;
- District Today;
- City Tomorrow;
- District Tomorrow.

---

# 3. Design Goals

Every Telegram Publication SHALL:

- present official information clearly;
- be easy to scan visually;
- remain structurally identical every day;
- scale from one address to hundreds of addresses;
- preserve the official territorial hierarchy;
- minimise unnecessary visual noise.

---

# 4. Publication Grammar

Every Publication SHALL follow the same structural grammar.

```
Publication
│
├── Territory Header
│
├── Planned Outages Section (optional)
│
├── Emergency Outages Section (optional)
│
└── Update Time (optional)
```

Inside every section the hierarchy SHALL be:

```
Section
│
├── Settlement
│
├── Time Interval
│
├── Street
│
└── Addresses
```

This hierarchy is normative.

---

# 5. Territory Header

The Publication SHALL begin with the Territory title.

Telegram Publications SHALL render Territory titles in uppercase.

Examples:

```
СТАРОКОСТЯНТИНІВ
```

```
САМЧИКІВСЬКИЙ СО
```

```
САМЧИКІВСЬКИЙ СТАРОСТИНСЬКИЙ ОКРУГ
```

The Publisher MAY use either the abbreviated or the full official district name.

---

# 6. Publication Sections

Publications may contain two sections.

**🟠 Планові знеструмлення**

**🔴 Аварійні знеструмлення**

The Planned section SHALL always appear before the Emergency section.

Empty sections SHALL NOT be rendered.

---

# 7. Settlement Ordering

Inside a Starosta District Publication, Settlements SHALL appear in the canonical order defined by TERRITORIAL_MODEL.md.

Alphabetical sorting is prohibited.

Parser ordering is prohibited.

---

# 8. Time Interval Ordering

Inside each Settlement, Time Intervals SHALL be sorted by their starting time.

Example:

```
🕘 09:00–17:00

🕘 10:00–12:00
```

---

# 9. Time Interval Formatting

## 9.1 Single-day interval

Single-day outages SHALL be rendered as:

```
🕘 09:00–17:00
```

---

## 9.2 Multi-day interval

If an outage spans multiple calendar days, the Publication SHALL include the complete dates.

Example:

```
🕘 з 7 липня 20:15 до 8 липня 16:45
```

---

# 10. Settlement Formatting

Settlement names SHALL be rendered in bold.

Examples:

```
<b>Великі Мацевичі</b>

<b>Самчики</b>

<b>Пеньки</b>
```

Administrative prefixes such as "с.", "м.", "смт." SHALL NOT be rendered.

---

# 11. Street Formatting

Street names SHALL begin on a new line.

Official Ukrainian abbreviations SHALL be preserved.

Examples:

```
вул. Козацька 1, 4, 5

пров. Садовий 1, 3, 8

майдан Незалежності 2
```

Addresses SHALL NOT be split across multiple lines.

---

# 12. Address Rules

Addresses belonging to the same street SHALL be rendered on one line.

Duplicate addresses are prohibited.

If one street appears in multiple Time Intervals, the street SHALL be repeated.

---

# 13. Canonical Templates

---

## Template A — City Today

### Purpose

Publishes today's information for the Administrative Centre.

### Required Fields

| Field | Required |
|---------|----------|
| Territory | Yes |
| Planned Section | Optional |
| Emergency Section | Optional |
| Update Time | Optional |

### Canonical Template

```
СТАРОКОСТЯНТИНІВ

🟠 Планові знеструмлення

...

🔴 Аварійні знеструмлення

...

Оновлено о HH:MM
```

🕘 09:00–17:00

вул. Кобєєва 4
вул. Колгоспна 3

вул. Садова

---

## Template C — City Tomorrow

### Purpose

Publishes tomorrow's planned outages for the Administrative Centre.

### Canonical Header

```
ПЛАНОВІ ЗНЕСТРУМЛЕННЯ
на 09 липня (завтра)
```

The remainder of the template SHALL follow Template A.

Temporary Publications SHALL NOT contain an Update Time.

---

## Template D — District Tomorrow

### Purpose

Publishes tomorrow's planned outages for one Starosta District.

The structure SHALL be identical to Template B.

Temporary Publications SHALL NOT contain an Update Time.

---

# 14. Validation Rules

A Publication SHALL NOT:

- render empty sections;
- change the order of sections;
- change the canonical Settlement order;
- sort Settlements alphabetically;
- duplicate addresses;
- merge multiple Starosta Districts into one Publication;
- omit Time Intervals;
- replace official street abbreviations.

---

# 15. Formatting Rules

Telegram formatting SHALL use only:

- **bold** (`<b>`).

Telegram block quotes MAY be used by future specifications where appropriate.

No other formatting SHALL be used.
