# TJS-001 — Journal Concept

Status: Draft (Чернетка)

Specification ID: TJS-001

Author: SvitloSk Project

---

# 1. Purpose

This specification defines the concept, mission, role and editorial philosophy of the official SvitloSk Telegram Journal.

It establishes the journal as an integral part of the SvitloSk ecosystem and serves as the foundation for all subsequent Telegram Journal specifications.

This document is normative.

---

# 2. Scope

This specification applies exclusively to the official SvitloSk Telegram channel.

It defines the principles governing the publication of information generated by the SvitloSk system.

---

# 3. Official Definition

The SvitloSk Telegram Journal is an automated public information journal that continuously analyzes official open data on electricity outages, transforms it into clear and structured information, and promptly delivers it to residents of the Starokostiantyniv Territorial Community.

The journal also serves as a chronological public archive of information processed by the SvitloSk system.

---

# 4. Mission

To provide residents with reliable, timely and understandable information about planned and emergency electricity outages based exclusively on official open data.

---

# 5. Vision

The Telegram Journal aims to become the primary public source of structured information about electricity outages within the Starokostiantyniv Territorial Community.

Its long-term value lies not only in informing residents in real time but also in preserving an accurate historical record of outage events.

---

# 6. Editorial Philosophy

The journal follows a simple principle:

> Every publication must increase clarity without changing the meaning of the official source.

The journal does not interpret, predict or alter official information.

It reorganizes and presents it in a form that is easier to understand.

---

# 7. Core Principles

The Telegram Journal is based on the following principles:

- Respect for official open data.
- Accuracy over speed.
- Clarity over complexity.
- Automation by default.
- Transparency of generated information.
- Consistency of publications.
- Continuous availability.
- Chronological integrity.
- Community-first approach.

---

# 8. Audience

The primary audience is the residents of the Starokostiantyniv Territorial Community.

Although the journal is publicly accessible, all editorial decisions shall prioritize the informational needs of this community.

---

# 9. Journal Identity

The Telegram Journal is not a news channel.

It is not a media outlet.

It is not a discussion platform.

It is an official automated public information journal of the SvitloSk project.

---

# 10. Role within SvitloSk

The Telegram Journal represents the public communication layer of the SvitloSk ecosystem.

It receives processed information from the Publication Engine and distributes it to subscribers in a structured and consistent manner.

---

# 11. Historical Archive

The Telegram Journal serves as the permanent public archive of publications related to the current day.

Daily publications concerning planned and emergency outages remain available as part of the historical record.

Temporary informational publications intended solely for advance notification may be edited or removed according to the publication lifecycle defined in subsequent specifications.
