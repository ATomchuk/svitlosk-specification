# Purpose

This document defines the permanent architectural role of the legacy Telegram specifications (TJS-001 through TJS-008) within the SvitloSk documentation system.

---

# Legacy Specification Status

## Current Role

The legacy TJS specifications (TJS-001 through TJS-008) served as **knowledge exploration documents** during the design phase of the Telegram documentation system. Their semantic content has been harvested and incorporated into the Semantic Foundation.

## Permanent Architectural Status

**Legacy Knowledge Sources**

The legacy specifications are officially classified as **Legacy Knowledge Sources**. They are:

- Historical records of the original design exploration
- Sources of semantic knowledge that has been harvested
- References for understanding the evolution of the Telegram documentation
- NOT normative specifications for future development
